Rising and volatile purchased feedstock and energy costs increase Dowapstas operating costs and add variability to earnings

The Company uses its feedstock flexibility and financial and physical hedging programs to reduce the negative effect of increases in these costs

As a result, increases in these costs could negatively impact the Companyapstas results of operations
